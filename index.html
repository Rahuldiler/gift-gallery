Frequently Asked Questions

Review the most commonly asked questions below.

How does Gift On Schedule work?

Gift On Schedule is an innovative web application that allows you to schedule gifts ahead of time. You can choose recipients, select thoughtful gifts, and provide necessary information about the recipients. The system ensures that the gifts are delivered on the selected date, so you never miss an important occasion again.

What is the purpose of the Quick Gift function?

The Quick Gift function is designed to simplify the gift-giving process. With just a few clicks, you can select a recipient, choose a gift, and provide their information to schedule the gift's delivery. It ensures that you can effortlessly send meaningful gifts without any hassle.

How does the Send by Link feature function?

The Send by Link feature is perfect for situations when you don't have the recipient's shipping address. It allows you to generate a unique gift link and send it via SMS, email, or any other preferred method. The recipient will click on the link and provide their details to receive the gift.

What is Autopilot, and how does it simplify the gifting process?

Autopilot is a time-saving feature that automates your gifting experience. You can schedule important dates and occasions in advance, and the system will choose the perfect gift within your budget. The gifts will be prepared and sent automatically, saving you time and effort.

Can I schedule gifts for future occasions using Gift On Schedule?

Absolutely! Gift On Schedule allows you to plan and schedule gifts for future occasions, ensuring your loved ones receive their gifts on time and with thoughtfulness.

Is Gift On Schedule suitable for both personal and business gifting needs?

Yes, Gift On Schedule caters to both personal and business gifting needs. Whether you want to surprise family and friends or send gifts to clients and employees, our platform has you covered.

How does the Gift Campaign feature facilitate bulk gifting?

The Gift Campaign feature enables you to send the same thoughtful gift to multiple recipients with ease. You can create a campaign, select recipients in bulk, and choose a budget-friendly gift. The system generates individual gift links for each recipient, allowing you to send them personally via SMS, email, or other methods.

Can I send gifts to recipients without knowing their shipping addresses?

Yes, you can! The Gift by Link feature allows you to send personalized gift links directly to recipients, bypassing the need for shipping addresses. You can send the links via various communication channels.

Is Gift On Schedule completely free to use?

Yes, Gift On Schedule is absolutely free to use. You only pay for the gifts you select to send. We accept all major credit cards, making it easy and convenient for you to pay for the gifts you choose.

Can I personalize the gifts I send through Gift On Schedule?

Absolutely! You can personalize the gifts you send by selecting from a variety of options available within the platform. Make your gifts unique and special for your recipients.

Is there a limit to the number of recipients I can include in a Gift Campaign?

There is no strict limit to the number of recipients you can include in a Gift Campaign. The system is designed to accommodate bulk gifting, making it suitable for various gifting scenarios.

What happens if a recipient does not redeem their gift link?

If a recipient does not redeem their gift link, you will not be charged. You only pay for the gifts that are actually redeemed, ensuring you get the most value out of your gifting efforts.

How does Gift On Schedule ensure that the chosen gifts fit within my budget?

When using Autopilot or the Gift Campaign feature, you set the budget for the gifts. The system then chooses gifts that align with the budget you have specified.

How far in advance can I schedule a gift?

With Gift On Schedule, you can plan ahead and schedule gifts up to 1 year in advance. Whether it's for a birthday, anniversary, or any special occasion, rest assured that your thoughtful gift will be delivered right on time, as per your chosen schedule.

Will I get a reminder of a scheduled gift?

Yes, Gift On Schedule ensures you stay informed about your scheduled gifts. After completing the checkout process, you will receive an initial confirmation of the scheduled gift. Additionally, to avoid any last-minute surprises, you will receive another reminder the day before the scheduled gift date. Rest assured, we want to make sure you never miss the opportunity to celebrate your special occasions with your thoughtful gifts.

What if I want to change the collection after the gift has been scheduled?

If you have scheduled a gift but it has not been sent yet, you have the option to make changes to the gift selection. Whether you want to switch to a different collection, choose an alternative item, or personalize the gift further, we're here to accommodate your preferences. Simply inform us about the changes you'd like to make before the scheduled delivery date, and we will ensure your updated gift reaches your recipient with care and thoughtfulness. With Gift On Schedule, we understand that life can be unpredictable, and we are committed to providing the flexibility you need to create the perfect gifting experience.

How long do I have to cancel a scheduled gift?

You have the flexibility to cancel a scheduled gift at any time before its status changes to "shipped." Whether you need to make last-minute changes or unforeseen circumstances arise, you can easily cancel the gift and make any necessary adjustments to ensure a thoughtful and timely gifting experience. At Gift On Schedule, we strive to provide you with the utmost convenience and peace of mind when it comes to managing your scheduled gifts.

Is my personal and payment information secure when using Gift On Schedule?

Yes, we take data security seriously. Your personal and payment information is protected through secure encryption and advanced security measures, ensuring a safe and trustworthy gifting experience.

How does the system ensure that gifts arrive on time for special occasions?

At Gift On Schedule, we take pride in our meticulous planning. We prepare all the gifts well in advance, approximately 12 days before the actual occasion. This allows us ample time to deliver them to any destination worldwide, utilizing cost-effective ground shipping options. For perishable items like flower arrangements that require quick delivery, we ensure next-day or even same-day shipping.

Scheduling your occasions ahead of time not only guarantees timely delivery but also helps you save money by avoiding last-minute expensive deals. Embrace the convenience of planning in advance with Gift On Schedule, and enjoy thoughtful gifting without the stress of rushed deliveries.
